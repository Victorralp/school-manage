# Quick Start: Export Results Feature

## For Teachers

### Step 1: Navigate to Results
1. Log in to your Teacher Dashboard
2. Click on the **"Results"** tab

### Step 2: Export
- Click **"Export PDF"** for a printable report
- Click **"Export Excel"** for data analysis

### Step 3: Use Your File
- PDF: Print or share via email
- Excel: Open in Excel/Google Sheets for analysis

## For School Administrators

### Step 1: Navigate to Performance
1. Log in to your School Dashboard
2. Click on the **"Performance"** tab

### Step 2: Export School-Wide Data
- Click **"Export PDF"** for school reports
- Click **"Export Excel"** for detailed analysis

### Step 3: Share or Analyze
- Share with board members
- Analyze performance trends
- Archive for records
